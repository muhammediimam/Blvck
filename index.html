FAQ — Questions & Answers

Questions

Everything you need to know
before we build your legacy.

BlvckCube is a luxury brand management firm. We build, position, and grow brands through precision strategy, bespoke creative direction, and full-spectrum marketing execution. We don't just run campaigns — we engineer authority. Everything we do is deliberate, structured, and calibrated for long-term market dominance.

We work with ambitious founders, established businesses, and premium brands across 7+ industries who are ready to dominate their market — not merely compete in it. Our clients are decisive, results-oriented, and understand that brand authority is a long-term investment, not a one-time transaction. If you're looking for cheap and fast, we are not the right fit.

Our core pillars are:

  1. Brand Strategy & Identity — naming, positioning, visual systems, and brand guidelines.
  2. Marketing & Campaign Management — full-funnel strategies, paid media, and growth execution.
  3. Content & Media Production — photography, video, copywriting, and editorial direction.
  4. Technology & Digital Presence — websites, platforms, and digital infrastructure.
  5. Full Brand Management Retainers — end-to-end ongoing brand stewardship.

Every engagement is bespoke. We don't replicate — we architect.

It begins with a free 15-minute discovery call — no commitment, no sales pitch. From there:

  1. We assess your brand, identify gaps and opportunities, and present a tailored proposal.
  2. Once aligned, we sign a contract with clearly defined scope, timeline, and deliverables.
  3. Execution proceeds in structured phases with weekly reporting and full transparency throughout.
  4. Final delivery is followed by a review session and optional handoff or ongoing retainer.

Timelines are scope-dependent and defined before any work begins:

  • Single-service projects run 2–4 weeks on average.
  • Full brand builds take 6–12 weeks depending on depth and client responsiveness.
  • Retainer clients operate on monthly cycles with defined weekly deliverables and quarterly performance reviews.

We do not rush work. Precision takes time — and time well spent is the foundation of authority.

Every engagement is custom-quoted based on scope and scale. We do not offer fixed pricing because no two brands are the same. Our proposals are itemized, fully transparent, and signed before any work begins. You will never receive a surprise invoice from BlvckCube. Indicative package tiers are available on our Services page.

Yes. We operate out of Cairo, Egypt and Canada, with active clients across the Middle East, North Africa, North America, and Europe. All client management, communication, and delivery is conducted remotely with the same standard of precision as on-site engagements. Distance is irrelevant. Results are not.

Project-based work has no minimum beyond the agreed scope of the signed contract. Retainer clients commit to a minimum of 3 months — sufficient time for strategy to take root and for measurable results to materialize. Brand authority is not built overnight, and we will not pretend otherwise.

We are not an agency — we are a firm. Agencies execute tasks. We build brands. The distinction is ownership: we treat every client's brand as if it were our own. The Cubes operate with the precision of consultants and the vision of artists. We move with urgency, report with clarity, and deliver with the kind of excellence that doesn't need to announce itself.
